This repository contains Power BI dashboards focused on HR analytics and people insights, designed to turn employee data into meaningful, decision ready views.

Reports Included

Monthly Income Analysis
Analyzes how factors such as department, education, overtime, business travel, and work life balance influence employee income.

Working Years Analysis
Examines employee distribution across total working years to understand workforce experience levels, identify concentration areas, and highlight potential gaps for workforce planning.

Power BI

Purpose

To demonstrate how HR data can be transformed into clear, actionable insights that support informed people and business decisions using Power BI.

More reports will be added as part of this HR analytics series.
